Focus Area

Edge Data Center Industry 4.0 Solutions

Smart Manufacturing

Smart Manufacturing processes improve performance and efficiencies, reduced downtime with predictive maintenance, improve processes and real time decision making, using seamless connectivity of devices/sensors , automation, data analytics with AI/ML. It results in flexible customization and fast timer to market.

Video Surveillance

Provides the capability to monitor and record live feed and perform processing such as Number plate recognition, movement detection and alarms, facial recognition, object & person search, fire arms detection, security breaches etc.

Digital Twin

A digital twin is a virtual model of a physical object, system, or process that uses real-time data to simulate and monitor its behavior. This can be used to identify any potential issues and performance optimization.

Smart Metering

Smart Metering helps in automatically capturing the usage information in near real time over wireless networks and automating the process of billing and subscriber management. This can help in taking real time action and usage management, energy quota management etc.

Private Wireless

Private Wireless Networks like 5G/ WiFi-6 & 7 within the industry premises are becoming increasingly important for connectivity and communication. The industrial devices need to communicate in real time and the data needs to be captures for further analytics.

Data Analytics

Data analytics and AI help to analyze large datasets with real timeinformation, improving decision-making processes and enabling better strategic planning. This approach can be used for predictive maintenance, Supply chain management, Energy management.
