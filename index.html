Why Edge Data Solutions?

Lower Latency

Edge data centers process data locally and hence the data need not travel across internet or long distances, thus saving time and minimizing latency, providing a better user experience.

Bandwidth Optimization

By processing the data locally, the data need not travel across the internet, hence saving in the critical bandwidth.

Regulatory Compliance

Data is retained locally and processed within it, ensuring any of the compliance requirements of data privacy and local data retention.

Better Security

Local processing of data helps to limit the exposure perimeter and improves the security of the data and systems and reduces the risk of cyber attacks.

Reliability

The Edge Data Center architecture helps to create adequate redundancies across multple Edge DCs and integration with public clouds, through proper DR planning.

Scalability

Edge Data Centers provide flexibility of quick scaling of infrastructure as and when requlred. Our unique approach of Hybrid architecture ensured we also provide burst capacity with integration of public clouds.

Cost optimization

Edge DCs inherently helps with cost optimization on multiple counts e.g.:

  • Less data processing
  • Reduced data transfer needs.
  • Less DG capacity needed
  • Smaller cyberattack surface.
  • Faster business decisions

Managed Services

Completely managed service offerings with requisite SLAs help organizations to manage their infrastructure in an efficient manner with quick go live of solutions, reducing manpower onboarding/training delay and costs.

Sustainability

Reducing energy requirements through:

  • Reduces unwarranted data movement across geographies, due to local processing of data.
  • Less data handling due to local processing.
  • Less capacity DGs with load spread across multiple Edge DCs.

Edge Data Center Industry 4.0 Solutions

Smart Manufacturing

Smart Manufacturing processes improve performance and efficiencies, reduced downtime with predictive maintenance, improve processes and real time decision making, using seamless connectivity of devices/sensors , automation, data analytics with AI/ML. It results in flexible customization and fast timer to market.

Video Surveillance

Provides the capability to monitor and record live feed and perform processing such as Number plate recognition, movement detection and alarms, facial recognition, object & person search, fire arms detection, security breaches etc.

Digital Twin

A digital twin is a virtual model of a physical object, system, or process that uses real-time data to simulate and monitor its behavior. This can be used to identify any potential issues and performance optimization.

Smart Metering

Smart Metering helps in automatically capturing the usage information in near real time over wireless networks and automating the process of billing and subscriber management. This can help in taking real time action and usage management, energy quota management etc.

Private Wireless

Private Wireless Networks like 5G/ WiFi-6 & 7 within the industry premises are becoming increasingly important for connectivity and communication. The industrial devices need to communicate in real time and the data needs to be captures for further analytics.

Data Analytics

Data analytics and AI help to analyze large datasets with the real timeinformation, improving decision-making processes and enabling better strategic planning. This approach can be used for predictive maintenance, Supply chain management, Energy management.
